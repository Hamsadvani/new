About Me

I am a physician‑researcher with over 15 years of international experience spanning the World Health Organization, the University of Oxford, and UNICEF. My work focuses on health systems strengthening, clinical research governance, and equitable access to medicines across the Global South.

After completing an MSc in Health Policy, Planning & Financing at the LSHTM and the London School of Economics, I have led multidisciplinary teams, coordinated high‑impact research agendas, and advised governments on pandemic preparedness and universal health coverage.

Today I am expanding my toolkit by integrating Generative AI into research workflows—from automated data analysis to custom chatbots—to accelerate evidence generation and policy translation.

  • Health Systems Consultant, WHO India – Essential medicines & vaccine access (2014‑16)

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week live online certification that equips students, faculty and researchers with no‑code AI tools to automate everyday tasks and unlock new insights.

  • Next cohort: 13 July 2025
  • Workshops: Fridays 15:00 GMT or Sundays 17:00 GMT (choose one)
  • Tuition: USD 250 per month × 2 months  — 1‑month refund guarantee

Key Learning Outcomes

Documents, Presentations & Websites

Create publication‑ready content with LLMs and build no‑code websites.

Automated Data Analysis

Use Julius, ChatGPT Code Interpreter and Windsurf to turn raw datasets into actionable reports.

GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Streamline literature reviews, sentiment analysis and knowledge synthesis with Elicit & LiteRev.

Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Design RAG‑powered assistants and integrate them via APIs or Botpress workflows.

AI Workflows for Task Automation

Reclaim hours by orchestrating cross‑platform automations using Lindy and Make.com.

Communication & Outreach

Generate visuals, videos and marketing assets to share research findings.

Capstone Project & Showcase

Deliver an end‑to‑end AI solution and present it to peers for feedback.
